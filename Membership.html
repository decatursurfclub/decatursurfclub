Membership

There is a one-time fee of $100 for all new memberships. This is in addition to the annual dues.

Download Membership Application >

 

A single membership is available for individuals who must be at least age 12. If you wish to change your Family Membership to a Single, we must be notified in writing of the one person to be using the membership.

Please remember that the Rules and Regulations require that the annual dues must be paid before a member or their family may use the club facilities.

A member whose dues are delinquent for a period of 90 days (July 1) or more shall automatically be dropped from membership without any compensation paid.

The babysitter fee is $100 per season. The babysitter pass cannot be used as a single membership. The babysitter must come with your children. If you baby sit a child – that child can only come with the member.
